# Appointment Scheduler & Store Calendar

## Role: Front Desk / Stylist
### Purpose: Managing store traffic, fitting rooms, and specialist availability.

The Appointment Workspace is the central hub for all scheduled visits, including fittings, measurements, pickups, and stylist consultations.

---

## The Store Scheduler

1. **Access**: Navigate to **Appointments** > **Scheduler**.
2. **Booking a Visit**:
   - Select a **Date** and **Time Slot**.
   - Choose the **Resource** (e.g., Suite A, Fitting Room 1, or a specific Stylist).
   - Search for the **Customer**. Use a minimum of 3 characters for the most accurate results.
3. **Linking to a Registry**: If the appointment is for a wedding party member, linking the **Wedding Registry** profile ensures the measurement data flows directly into the party dashboard.
4. **Walk-ins**: Use the "Quick-Add" slot for immediate arrivals to track store conversion and resource utilization.

---

## Managing Conflicts

1. **Conflict Board**: Navigate to **Appointments** > **Conflicts** to view overlapping bookings.
2. **Resolving Overlaps**:
   - Double-bookings are highlighted in amber.
   - Drag and drop appointments to adjacent free slots.
   - Add a note if a conflict is intentional (e.g., shared fitting room with staggered start times).

---

## Terminology: Weddings vs. Appointments

- **Wedding Registry Calendar**: Focused on party-specific milestones (e.g., Groom's First Fitting, Final Pickup Window).
- **Store Scheduler (Appointments)**: The universal operational calendar for everyone entering the building.

---

## Common Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Customer Not Found** | Ensure the name is spelled correctly or try searching by Phone Number. |
| **Slot Won't Save** | Verify that the room or specialist is not marked as "Busy" or "Out of Office". |
| **Timezone Mismatch** | Check the Store Settings to ensure the system clock matches your local region. |

> [!TIP]
> Use the **Day View** for high-traffic Saturdays to monitor room turnover, and the **Week View** for long-term planning.
