# POS Inventory Lookup & SKU Search

## Role: Sales Floor / Cashier
### Purpose: Real-time price, availability, and variant checks at the counter.

The POS Inventory interface is optimized for high-speed lookups during a customer consultation, providing immediate access to stock levels and variant data.

---

## Using the Inventory Screen

1. **Access**: In POS Mode, select the **Inventory** (box) icon on the left rail.
2. **Search**: Enter at least **two characters** to begin a search. You can search by **SKU**, **Style Name**, **Vendor Code**, or **Color**.
3. **Adding to Cart**:
   - Tapping a product with a single variant adds it immediately to the active sale.
   - Products with multiple variants (e.g., sizes/widths) will open a **Variant Picker**. Select the desired size and tap **Add to Sale**.
4. **Load More**: If the item isn't in the initial results, tap **Load More Products** at the bottom of the list.

---

## Reading Availability Data

- **Available for Sale**: This reflects (Stock on Hand) - (Reserved Orders).
- **Out of Stock**: Items with 0 availability will display a clipboard icon. **Action**: Offer a **Special Order** or check for incoming shipments in the **Inventory Overview** (Back Office).
- **Reserved Only**: If an item is in the building but reserved for another customer, it will not be available for new sales.

---

## Special & Custom Items

- **Custom MTM**: Items booked using the `CUSTOM` keyword do not appear in the standard POS Inventory list until they are received and converted into physical instances.
- **Price Mismatch**: If the shelf price differs from the scanner price, follow your store SOP for manager overrides. Do not promise digital/website pricing without approval.

---

## Common Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| **"Search Spins"** | Use a more specific keyword or the exact SKU from the tag. |
| **Incorrect On-Hand** | The "Receiving" batch may not have been posted yet. Coordinate with the Inventory Lead. |
| **No Results** | Try a broader search or verify the item in the Back Office **Product List**. |

> [!TIP]
> Use the **SKU Scan** whenever possible to avoid accidental variant selection.
