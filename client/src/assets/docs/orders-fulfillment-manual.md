# Fulfillment Command Center

## Role: Operations / Fulfillment Specialist
### Purpose: Managing logistical throughput and inventory readiness.

The Fulfillment Command Center is the logistical heart of Riverside OS, prioritizing which orders need immediate action to meet customer deadlines.

---

## Accessing the Workspace

1. Navigate to **Orders** > **Fulfillment** in the Back Office.
2. The dashboard automatically sorts by urgency, placing **Rush** and **Overdue** items at the top.

---

## Status Indicators (Action Bar)

- **Ready**: Every line item on the order is physically in stock and ready for the customer. **Action**: Move to the pickup shelf and send a "Ready for Pickup" notification.
- **Rush**: Orders with an expedited timeline or manual "Rush" flag. **Action**: Process these as top priority.
- **Due Soon**: Items with a "Need By" date within the next **4 days**. **Action**: Verify shipping/vendor arrival status.
- **Blocked**: Orders with no activity for **14+ days**. **Action**: Investigate for missing vendor costs or shipping delays.

---

## The Fulfillment Queue

The queue provides high-density visibility into logistical state:
- **🔴 Rush Badge**: Critical priority.
- **🟡 Due Soon**: Shift priority.
- **🟢 Fullfillment Ready**: Logistics complete.
- **⚪ Blocked**: Stale or error state.

---

## Operational Workflow

1. **Morning Sweep**: Start with the **Ready** list. Clearing these orders recognizes revenue and frees up physical inventory space.
2. **Rush Management**: Monitor the **Action Board** for new Rush arrivals. 
3. **Queue Cleanup**: Review the **Blocked** list daily. If an item is delayed at the vendor, add a note to the order so the front desk can inform the customer.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Missing Expected Order** | Ensure the transaction wasn't incorrectly marked as a "Draft" or "Parked Sale". |
| **"Ready" but items missing** | A staff member may have manually toggled fulfillment. Reverse the status and conduct a physical search. |
| **Blocked for 30+ days** | Coordinate with Procurement to verify if the vendor has cancelled the item. |

> [!TIP]
> Use the **Direct Open** button to jump straight into the transaction record for rapid status updates.
