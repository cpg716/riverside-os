The Flash Sales Report provides a brief one-page synopsis of sales activity at one 
or more stores for a specified period of time. Flash Sales shows the total sales, 
number of tickets, average sales amount per ticket, and the gross profit for each 
store during the reported period.  
You can also show percent of total values (e.g., Downtown store was 22% of total 
sales), or include a pie chart for an at-a-glance understanding of the data presented.  
The report can be ordered by store, by sales, by profit, or by any of the reported 
columns, such as sales last week, profit this year, etc. You may choose to report 
information for a comparative period.  
Flash Sales is a very powerful reporting and analysis tool that lets you define and 
present your own analysis of store operations. If you don’t want to use the standard 
reported columns, you can select to print up to five analysis columns from a list that 
includes sales, cost, returns, discounts, profit, profit percent, quantity sold, number 
of lines, and average ticket amount.
