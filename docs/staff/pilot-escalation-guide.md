# Pilot escalation guide

**Audience:** All pilot staff.

**Purpose:** What to do when a live-store workflow becomes uncertain.

---

## Blocked checkout

Cashier:

1. Stop retrying after one safe retry.
2. Park the sale if possible.
3. Note customer, amount, time, register, and message.
4. Call manager.

Manager:

1. Check whether payment was attempted.
2. Review recovery/payment status.
3. Decide whether to resume, retry, park, or document.
4. Do not close register until recovery is clear or assigned.

---

## Refund uncertainty

Cashier:

1. Stop before tendering refund.
2. Keep original transaction visible.
3. Call manager.

Manager:

1. Verify original transaction.
2. Verify customer and item.
3. Verify return window and tender.
4. Decide refund, exchange, store credit, or no action.
5. Review before close.

---

## Wrong wedding member risk

Consultant:

1. Stop pickup/edit.
2. Confirm party name and event date.
3. Confirm member identity.
4. Call wedding lead/manager.

Manager:

1. Review member record, balance, notes, and garment status.
2. Correct the record if needed.
3. Document if pickup was changed or reopened.

---

## Payment uncertainty

Cashier:

1. Do not run the card again immediately.
2. Keep customer and amount visible.
3. Call manager.

Manager:

1. Check terminal/provider outcome.
2. Check ROS transaction/payment state.
3. If unclear, treat as unresolved payment review.
4. Document next action before close.

---

## Offline replay issue

Staff:

1. Do not clear browser storage.
2. Do not recreate the sale unless manager approves.
3. Record customer, amount, time, and register.

Manager:

1. Review offline recovery.
2. Resolve or assign.
3. Register close waits for recovery clarity.

---

## Inventory mismatch

Staff:

1. Do not adjust stock casually.
2. Confirm SKU/size/location.
3. Call manager for adjustment or receiving review.

Manager:

1. Determine reason: count, damage, receiving, return, or customer-service issue.
2. Require a clear note.
3. Review at end of day.

---

## Close blocker

Closing staff:

1. Read the blocker.
2. Do not force close.
3. Call manager if not already manager.

Manager:

1. Identify owner.
2. Resolve if possible.
3. If unresolved, document reason, owner, and next review time.

---

## QBO failure or warning

Staff:

1. Do not post or approve around the warning.
2. Notify accounting/manager.

Accounting/manager:

1. Review warning type.
2. Resolve mapping, timing, or failed posting.
3. Post only when ready.

---

## RMS reporting issue

Staff:

1. Do not mark reported unless follow-up was actually completed.
2. Notify manager/sales support.

Manager:

1. Review RMS Charge record.
2. Confirm R2S follow-up.
3. Assign exception or reconciliation follow-up.
