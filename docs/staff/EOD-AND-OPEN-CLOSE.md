# Open, close, and end-of-day (staff narrative)

**Audience:** Cashiers and leads.  
**Purpose:** Shift-shaped checklist that complements **screen** guides. **Merge this with your store’s written SOP** ([STORE-SOP-TEMPLATE.md](STORE-SOP-TEMPLATE.md)); numbers here are generic.

---

## Before open (Back Office + floor)

1. Sign in to **Back Office** — [00-getting-started.md](00-getting-started.md).
2. **Operations → Dashboard:** weather, floor team, notifications, **My tasks** — [operations-home.md](operations-home.md).
3. Complete **opening tasks** if assigned — [pos-tasks.md](pos-tasks.md).
4. Enter **POS mode** when ready to use the till — [register-tab-back-office.md](register-tab-back-office.md).

---

## Open register

1. From Back Office **POS** → **Register** (subsection), tap **Enter POS**, then complete **open till** / keypad flow per **store SOP**.
2. Confirm header shows **active session** (not **No Active Session**).
3. **POS → Dashboard:** quick scan of session widgets you are allowed to see — [pos-dashboard.md](pos-dashboard.md).

---

## During the day

- **Sales:** [pos-register-cart.md](pos-register-cart.md).
- **Lookup / add from browse:** [pos-inventory.md](pos-inventory.md).
- **Overrides:** manager PIN when over cap — see [staff-administration.md](staff-administration.md) (**Discount caps**).
- **Stuck errors:** [ERROR-AND-TOAST-GUIDE.md](ERROR-AND-TOAST-GUIDE.md).

---

- **Shift handoff** without closing drawer: [../STAFF_TASKS_AND_REGISTER_SHIFT.md](../STAFF_TASKS_AND_REGISTER_SHIFT.md).

---

## Close register

1. Finish or park sales per policy — no abandoned tenders.
2. Run **close / Z** on **Register #1** when your store uses a **till group** (satellite **#2+** lanes do not have their own Z-close in POS — one **Z** closes every lane in the shift).
3. **Professional Z-Report**: The closing report will automatically route to your assigned **Report Station** (Full-Page/Audit) rather than the receipt printer.
4. **Cash:** count and reconcile per **dual control** SOP; discrepancies → manager immediately ([STORE-SOP-TEMPLATE.md](STORE-SOP-TEMPLATE.md)).
5. **Close register** in software when SOP says (may differ from simply exiting POS).

---

## After close (Back Office)

- **Transactions → Open Transactions:** clear stragglers or document — [transactions-back-office.md](transactions-back-office.md).
- **Insights** or **QBO** (if used): per accounting schedule — [insights-back-office.md](insights-back-office.md), [qbo-bridge.md](qbo-bridge.md).

---

## When to stop and get a manager

- **Cash** over/short beyond tolerance.
- **Void** or **refund** outside your training.
- Repeated **500** errors during close — [ERROR-AND-TOAST-GUIDE.md](ERROR-AND-TOAST-GUIDE.md).
