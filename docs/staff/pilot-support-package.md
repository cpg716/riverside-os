# Pilot support package

**Audience:** Owner, pilot lead, manager on duty.

**Purpose:** Printable launch-day and daily support materials for controlled pilot execution.

---

## Launch-day checklist

Before opening:

- Confirm pilot build/version.
- Confirm database backup completed.
- Confirm rollback build or fallback procedure is available.
- Confirm registers open.
- Confirm receipt printer works.
- Confirm scanner works.
- Confirm terminal/payment path is ready.
- Confirm RMS and QBO owners are available.
- Print quick guides and escalation tree.
- Assign issue log owner.
- Assign daily review time.
- Confirm manager-only workflows.

During opening hour:

- Watch first checkout.
- Watch first customer lookup.
- Watch first parked sale if used.
- Watch first refund/exchange if used.
- Watch first wedding pickup if used.
- Watch first alteration intake if used.

Before close:

- Review issue log.
- Review unresolved workflows.
- Review RMS and QBO.
- Review refunds and parked sales.
- Confirm register close owner.

---

## Manager-on-duty checklist

Every shift:

- Know who owns checkout, refunds, close, RMS, QBO, wedding, alterations, receiving, and inventory.
- Keep issue log open or printed.
- Stop repeated unsafe retries.
- Approve fallback work.
- Review any Manager Access use.
- Review customer-facing incidents before staff leave.
- Make the daily Green/Yellow/Red decision.

---

## Pilot communication template

Use before the shift:

> Today we are running Riverside OS under pilot controls. If payment, refund, close, pickup, RMS, QBO, receiving, or inventory feels uncertain, pause and call a manager. Do not guess, do not retry payments blindly, and do not release garments with unclear balance or identity. Log slowdowns and repeated questions so we can fix training and procedures.

Use after a workflow issue:

> We are pausing this workflow until a manager reviews it. Keep the customer/item/amount visible, save any receipt or screenshot, and write down what happened.

## Known watch-items list

Watch closely:

- Refund/exchange confidence.
- Register close warnings and **Unresolved Issues at Close** evidence.
- Offline recovery and parked sales.
- RMS account selection and reconciliation.
- QBO staging and approval.
- Wedding member identity, pickup readiness, and balance due.
- Alteration intake that is saved but not scheduled.
- Receiving drafts and physical inventory publish.
- Hardware interruptions: printer, scanner, payment terminal.
- Staff hesitation or repeated lookup fatigue.
