# Section 3 Reporting Risk Register

Date: 2026-05-12

This register tracks reporting and accounting-trust risks for Section 3 of the post-release operationalization package. Status reflects the current documented and tested evidence, not future intent.

| Risk ID | Area | Description | Severity | Status | Evidence / current coverage | Remaining action | Owner / signoff role |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| RPT-001 | Reporting/QBO parity | Duplicated math between reporting and QBO can drift across revenue, COGS, returns, and liability reporting. | Critical | Partially mitigated | Daily Sales, margin, sales-pivot, and QBO restock contracts exist for the highest-risk paths. | Keep reporting-trust and QBO contracts paired when accounting formulas change. | Engineering + Accounting |
| RPT-002 | Report freshness | Stale report logic could keep showing outdated semantics after accounting logic changes. | High | Partially mitigated | Focused reporting-trust contracts now cover several critical routes. | Add contract coverage for any newly trusted report before rollout signoff. | Engineering |
| RPT-003 | Booked vs fulfilled semantics | Booked activity could be confused with fulfilled/recognized revenue. | Critical | Mitigated for scoped routes | Fulfilled-basis reporting trust coverage exists; docs preserve the distinction. | Continue manual review for new report surfaces. | Accounting |
| RPT-004 | Fulfillment classification | Incorrect fulfillment classification can place revenue in the wrong recognition period. | Critical | Partially mitigated | Fulfilled-basis register/sales reporting coverage exists for controlled scenarios. | Add targeted coverage if new fulfillment types or pickup states are introduced. | Operations + Accounting |
| RPT-005 | UTC vs store-local date drift | Reports using UTC windows can disagree with QBO/register store-local business dates near local midnight. | Critical | Mitigated for scoped margin and sales-pivot routes | Store-local date coverage exists for margin-pivot and sales-pivot fulfilled reporting. | Watch remaining reports for UTC bucket patterns before treating them as trusted. | Engineering |
| RPT-006 | Returned quantity overstatement | Margin/COGS reports can overstate revenue, COGS, and gross margin if returned quantities are ignored. | Critical | Mitigated for margin-pivot | Margin effective-quantity coverage exists after partial return. | Preserve returned-effective-quantity rule in future report changes. | Engineering + Accounting |

| RPT-009 | Retail value confusion | Product Hub retail value could be mistaken for accounting inventory value. | High | Mitigated for labeling/coverage | Product Hub wording and inventory audit coverage distinguish retail value on hand. | Keep staff/accounting docs clear that retail value is operational. | Operations |
