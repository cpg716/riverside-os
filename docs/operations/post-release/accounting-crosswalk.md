## Guardrails

- Product Hub retail value and Inventory Control Board current-cost value are operational displays, not canonical accounting inventory valuation.
- QBO proposal balancing is accounting evidence, not approval. Accounting must still review and approve/post.
- Counterpoint imported tax warnings and signoff evidence are not tax filing proof.
- Booked activity, fulfilled/recognition activity, and return-day contra activity must remain separate in reporting reviews.
